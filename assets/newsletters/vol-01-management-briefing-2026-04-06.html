CRM UPDATE

Go High Level — Pipeline & Lead Automation

Our Go High Level CRM has been upgraded with a fully automated lead pipeline. Warm replies from our outreach campaigns are now pulled in automatically, classified, and placed directly into the Call Center pipeline at the Call Queue stage — assigned to an agent and ready for follow-up. Every lead arrives with the original reply attached as a note, so agents have full context before the first call. Disposition tracking is in place across all pipeline stages: agents tag each outcome (DQ reason, lost reason, or booking), and the system handles the rest. New warm replies are checked every five minutes and distributed via round-robin so no lead sits idle. The days of raw CSV lists and manual imports are behind us.
