Every lead now tells a clear story. When an agent disqualifies a contact, they select exactly one reason. Same for lost deals. 4  /  QUICK TIPS FOR AGENTS

1

Read the note before you call

Every lead has the property owner's original reply saved. Mention what they said and you'll build instant rapport.
2

Move stages in real time

Don't batch-update at end of day. Move the lead as the conversation happens so supervisors always see the real picture.
3

Tag before you move

If you're disqualifying or closing a lead, add the reason tag first. The system will alert your supervisor if it's missing.
4

Keep your calendar current

New leads are assigned based on your availability. If your calendar isn't up to date, leads go to the next agent.
