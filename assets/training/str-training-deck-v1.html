Stages 1 & 2
Call Queue & Attempted

📞 Call Queue

  • Every new lead starts here
  • Goal: Contact within minutes
  • Read the lead's notes before you dial
  • Check source tags for context
  • If they answer → qualify
    If not → move to Attempted

📷 Attempted / No Answer

  • Called but no pickup — that's okay
  • Try again at different times of day
  • Leave a voicemail referencing their inquiry
  • After multiple attempts: tag cc:lost_no_response
Most deals are won in the follow-up. Don't give up after one try.
Stage 3 — Overflow

👤 What You Do With VAPI Leads

Stage 4
Connected — Disqualified

You reached the lead but they don't qualify. Select exactly one DQ reason tag before moving:

cc:dq_no_properties No STR properties
cc:dq_wrong_market Outside our service area
cc:dq_not_interested Not interested
cc:dq_already_managed Already with another PM
cc:dq_budget Pricing doesn't fit
cc:dq_wrong_contact Not the decision maker

⚠ Add the DQ tag BEFORE moving the lead. The system will flag a warning if it's missing.

Stages 5 & 6
Qualified & Booked

🎯 Qualified — Not Booked

  • Prospect qualifies — interested but uncommitted
  • Goal: Get the appointment scheduled
  • Use calendar links to remove friction
  • Address objections: timing, pricing, "need to think"
  • Follow up consistently — this is a hot lead

📅 Booked

  • Appointment is officially scheduled
  • Confirmations & reminders go out automatically
  • Prep: review property details & market data
  • No-show? Reschedule promptly
  • Cancellation? Understand why & re-engage

Your Routine
A Day in the Life

☀ Morning

Check Call Queue — prioritize newest leads. Review Attempted leads needing follow-up. Re-engage no-shows from yesterday.

☂ Midday

Continue outbound calls. Update pipeline stages in real-time. Apply DQ or Lost tags immediately — don't wait.

🌅 Afternoon

Push Qualified leads toward appointments. Check Contract Sent leads for questions. Log all calls and notes in the CRM.

🌙 End of Day

Confirm all leads are in the correct stage. No lead sits in Call Queue overnight uncontacted. Update calendar for tomorrow.

Best Practices
Tips for Success

1. Speed to Lead

Contact new leads within 5 minutes — the #1 predictor of conversion.

2. Read the Notes

Personalize every call using the lead's own words and context.

3. Follow Up Relentlessly

80% of deals require 5+ follow-ups. Stay persistent.

4. Tag & Escalate

Honest tagging drives strategy. Loop in your supervisor on complex objections.
